# Code of Conduct

QuizForge AI is committed to providing a respectful and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and professional
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be constructive when giving feedback
- Support beginners and new contributors

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or abusive language
- Spam or disruptive behavior

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may take appropriate action for violations, including removing content or blocking users.

If you face any issue, please contact the maintainer privately.
